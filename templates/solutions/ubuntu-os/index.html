Setting the standard for security and support

Ubuntu is secure by design and dependable in a fast-paced security environment.

Security in-depth, at the platform level

Ubuntu gives you a secure platform to build on from the ground up. We use available hardware roots of trust, such as TPMs, for full disk encryption. Combined with hardening for the early boot process, and Apparmor for confinement of applications, we ensure a reduced attack surface and security throughout your stack.

Ubuntu also supports the latest confidential computing technology, helping you protect data while itâ€™s being processed.

A release cadence you can depend on

Benefit from always knowing when updates are coming: an interim release every 6 months to test the latest features and a new Long Term Support (LTS) version every 2 years. With Ubuntu, you know where you stand.

Stability through long term support

Our LTS releases are supported for 5 years as standard, allowing you to focus more on innovation and less on maintenance. Upgrade to Pro to get a total of 12 years in expanded security maintenance.

A single subscription for security and compliance

Ubuntu gives you secure access to the widest open source software library. Reduce your average CVE exposure from 98 days to 1 on average and comply with the most stringent security regulations.
