Why choose Canonical?

We deliver security maintenance for the whole stack: from the OS, to infrastructure, to containers and their dependencies.

Start with Ubuntu's securely designed foundation. Adopt Ubuntu Pro, a subscription for enterprise-grade security, support and compliance, and simplify vulnerability management for all your open source.

Compliance standards

We simplify compliance with automation tools and hardening profiles, including FIPS 140-2 and 140-3, CMMC, FedRamp, DISA-STIG and CIS.

Achieve compliance everywhere with Ubuntu Pro – on-premise, in air-gapped environments, on the edge, and on public clouds.
