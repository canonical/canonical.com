Why choose Canonical's Managed MongoDB®

Streamline operations

Offload critical MongoDB operational service delivery to Canonical, so that your internal team can focus more on your business goals.

High availability

High uptime, backed by an SLA for peace of mind. We guarantee 99.9% availability of the MongoDB service. Our deep expertise in open source software underpins your SLA.

Secure and compliant

Canonical ensures that MongoDB stays secure by providing patches for Common Vulnerabilities Exposure (CVE). In addition, Charmed MongoDB includes security features such as authentication and authorisation, Transport Layer Security (TLS), user management and password rotation.

Innovate at scale with your data

Canonical's managed services for MongoDB scale efficiently — so your MongoDB service remains stable and exceptionally cost effective as your business grows.
