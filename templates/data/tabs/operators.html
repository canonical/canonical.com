Lifecycle management on all clouds

Our software operators enable you to use a single codebase to automate the lifecycle management of your data applications across public clouds, Kubernetes, virtual (VM) and bare metal machines. This includes supporting services like observability, identity, secrets, and more.

This is possible thanks to Juju and charms. Juju is an open source orchestration engine that enables the deployment, integration, and lifecycle management of applications at any scale, on any infrastructure.
