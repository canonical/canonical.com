Early Careers
at Canonical

At Canonical, we believe that talent is universally distributed, but opportunity is not. Our early careers program is driven by the desire to create a globally accessible, structured, and scalable pathway that nurtures potential, fosters continuous development, and aligns with our remote-first, open source culture. We call this the "Journey to Prof I."

It includes a defined three-tiered progression model (Graduate → Associate → Prof I), embedded feedback and promotion cycles, ownership, and impact from day one.

This initiative ensures that our early-career hires are not only set up for long-term career success, but also empowered to thrive. It is a core part of Canonical's commitment to inclusive, future-focused talent development.

Interested in applying? Find out more about our hiring process.
