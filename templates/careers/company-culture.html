Built on trust

As a remote-first organisation, trust is our currency. At Canonical, you get unprecedented flexibility in how you organise your day and freedom to explore new roles within the company. We trust each other to be organised and effective in a distributed workplace. If you value teamwork and are committed to being a good counterpart to colleagues around the world, you will love working here.

Life at Canonical

Travel and Lifestyle

We are remote but connected. The walls that exist in a co-located environment don’t exist here. Find out how we collaborate and what we value in our fully-distributed team.

Diversity
and hiring

We believe that talent is evenly distributed around the world. No matter where you were born, what you look like or how you like to dress. Our commitment to diversity informs our hiring process.

Progression

Your career at Canonical can progress in many ways. We celebrate growth in many forms. We encourage team members to find their passion and invest in that.

Sustainability

As a technology company, we know our work and our products have an environmental impact. Optimising our products and operations to minimise our impact is important to us.
