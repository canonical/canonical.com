We set you up for success

{{ lite_video(video_id="FrkLlt2tK3Y", video_title="We set you up for success at Canonical") }}

Your growth at Canonical is engineered for your success through a structured onboarding journey. This includes an in-person induction, enabling you to make personal connections and understand how we operate across different areas of the company.

You'll also be paired with a regional colleague outside of your immediate team during pre-onboarding through our Peer Buddy program. They'll be a dedicated point of contact for practical support, insider tips, and a friendly welcome before you even arrive.

You own your career development and we provide you with the support to help accelerate it.

Our mentorship program matches you with experienced team members across the company. They'll offer invaluable advice, insights from their own experiences, and practical guidance to support your continuous growth.

The technology landscape moves fast. We'll help you keep up.

You get a personal USD 2,000 learning budget annually to develop professional and technical skills for your future.

Whether you choose to use your budget improving coding skills, upskilling through certifications, attending industry conferences, learning a new language, or building communication and leadership skills, you'll have the opportunity to intentionally shape your professional development.

Local meetups

Team member led Resource Groups

Water Cooler Buddy program

Even in a globally distributed, remote-first company, you can still find your local Canonical community. From casual meetups to gatherings on special occasions, local meetups offer a chance to meet with coworkers living in your region and grow your network offline.

Connect with people around shared interests, experiences, and goals. Join Canonical Pride, the Women's Resource Group, Sustainable Growth, and more. If there's a community you'd love to see, you can help to create it yourself.

Globally distributed work doesn't mean working in a silo. To bridge the gap between our in-person sprints, we host a virtual Water Cooler Buddy program. This initiative brings people together, matching you with peers internationally to help you build meaningful connections.
