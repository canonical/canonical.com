Working here

Projects you'll work on

The design team at Canonical crafts the user experiences of sophisticated technical specialists across a wide range of industries. Driven by the precision craft of our design system. Canonical delivers Ubuntu, and a range of products – for engineers, admins and enterprises – to help businesses move to an open source stack.

Your opportunity is to shape the user experience across the enterprise infrastructure and application operations landscape. There are challenges at every layer of the stack – from bare metal data centre operations to virtualisation infrastructure and cloud, to the desktop, to systems management, to serverless and embedded connected devices.

Being part of the team

Canonical designers work on a range of products, using a centralised design lead – design system to deliver a consistent experience across a very wide range of web interfaces.

Our team has specialists in visual and UX design, and all aspects of web engineering. We collaborate, as product design squads, with globally distributed development engineering teams. Designers need to be effective in working collaboratively in a digital space. We travel to engineering sprints and summits for face-to-face time.

Who you are

We count on our team to bring together deep design-thinking, technical domain knowledge, with great instincts, through a rigorous process – to shape products for a very demanding audience. You'll fit in if you have equal appetites for design, innovation and cutting-edge technology.
